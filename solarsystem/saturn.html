Saturn

The second largest planet in the solar system after Jupiter, and the sixth in terms of distance from the Sun. Saturn is a gas giant with a radius approximately nine times that of Earth. Saturn's density is one-eighth that of Earth, but its volume is 95 times that of Earth.Saturn's upper atmosphere is mostly mild and calm. Nevertheless, long-lasting atmospheric phenomena can also be observed. Saturn's wind speed is 1,800 km/h, which is stronger than that of Jupiter and weaker than that of Neptune.
