Venus

It completes its movement around the Sun in 224.7 Earth days. Compared to other planets located in the solar system, it has the longest rotation period around its axis (243 days) and, unlike other planets, it rotates in the opposite direction. The atmospheric pressure of the planet is 92 times greater than the atmospheric pressure on Earth. This indicator corresponds to the pressure indicator of water at a depth of 900 m on Earth.
